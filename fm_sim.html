Explore how different modulating signals affect the frequency spectrum.

The complex baseband FM signal $$s(t)$$ is generated using the formula:

$$s(t) = e^{j (\phi(t) + \phi_0)} = \cos(\phi(t) + \phi_0) + j\sin(\phi(t) + \phi_0)$$

where the modulated phase $$\phi(t)$$ is:

$$\phi(t) = 2\pi f_c t + 2\pi \int_{0}^{t} f_{\Delta} m(\tau) d\tau$$
